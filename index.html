Become a DGCA Certified Drone Pilot

As per Government regulations, a DGCA-certified Drone Pilot Certificate is mandatory for all commercial drone operations. Launch your career with Vayuratha, a DGCA-authorized Remote Pilot Training Organization (RPTO).

Small Class (2-25 KG) RPC Training Program (6 Days)

  • • DAYS 1-2: THEORY
    Drone Rules 2021, Principles of Flight, ATC Procedures, Meteorology, and more.
  • • DAY 3: HANDS-ON ASSEMBLY & SIMULATION
    Basic drone assembly, maintenance, and flight simulator training.
  • • DAYS 4-6: PRACTICAL FLYING
    Master flight skills with instructor-led and solo flying sessions, followed by a final practical test.
